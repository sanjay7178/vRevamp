At Null Chapter we develop web and mobile applications that help other students to navigate through their college life in a much smoother manner. vRevamp is not an official entity that falls under the management of VIT and hence the data produced here isn't official and is just a personal opinion shared by various individuals of the same college.

General Summary

  • We do not collect any personal data except for an email ID.
  • In order to avoid excessive data collection, we follow OTP based verification so that we don't have access to the additional information shared when logged in using third party authentication providers like Google.
  • Sharing of user's name is completely optional and is used only for a personalised user experience.
  • We ask for a college specific email ID in-order to avoid access from external users.
  • None of the data mentioned above is used for any sort of commercial use.
